Return & Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you have any concerns about your order, we are here to help.

1. Returns

  • We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery.
  • To be eligible for a return, the item must be in the same condition that you received it.
  • Special customized/engraved items are not eligible for return unless there is a manufacturing defect.

2. Refunds

  • Once we receive and inspect your return, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.
  • If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 7–10 working days.
  • Shipping charges are generally non-refundable.

3. Replacement for Non-Floating Stones

Our unique promise: If your Ram Setu stone does not float on water as promised, we will provide a free replacement or a full refund, including shipping costs. Please provide a video proof of the stone not floating to initiate this process.

4. Cancellation Policy

  • You can cancel your order within 12 hours of placement or until it has been shipped, whichever is earlier.
  • For cancelled orders that were prepaid, the refund will be processed to the original payment method.

5. How to Initiate a Return

To initiate a return or refund, please email us at info@ramsetudivinestones.com with your order number and the reason for the return. Our team will guide you through the next steps.
